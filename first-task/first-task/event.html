What is event management software?

Every event organizer needs a personal assistant around to help manage
event activities and stay on track before,during,and after an event.Event
management software(EMS) does just that.It's a virtual assistant that enables
organizers to plan and execute an entire event,including promotions,
registrations,and even analyzing the event's performance.
